How it works

Use QuAI without changing apps.

01

Select text anywhere

Highlight a sentence, paragraph, email draft, chat message, or document section.

02

Press your shortcut

Open QuAI from your keyboard shortcut and choose the writing style you need.

03

Review and apply

Check what changed, refine the result if needed, then copy it or replace the original.

Built for repeated use

Everything you need for everyday writing.

Proofread and rewrite

Fix grammar, make writing sound more professional, shorten text, or expand rough notes.

Translate quickly

Turn selected text into another language from the same shortcut you use for proofreading.

Review every change

Compare the original and improved version side by side before replacing anything.

One-step shortcuts

Assign your favorite actions to hotkeys when you want proofreading or rewriting to happen instantly.

Your choice of AI

Use online AI with your own key, or download an offline model for private local writing help.

Custom writing styles

Create and reorder your own styles so QuAI matches the way you write every day.

Privacy by default

Your writing stays under your control.

Offline mode can process writing on your device.

Online AI is optional and only works after you add your own key.

Usage analytics are off by default and never include your writing.
